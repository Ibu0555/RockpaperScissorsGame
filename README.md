# 🚀 Welcome to the **Ultimate Futuristic Rock Paper Scissors Game!**

A classic game, reinvented for the digital age — now with a gamified, interactive, and visually stunning experience!  
Play against the AI, track your progress, and unlock your inner champion.

---

## 🎮 **Game Features**

- ⚡️ **Classic RPS Gameplay**: Battle the computer in real-time.
- 🤖 **Smart AI Opponent**: AI picks its move—fair and unpredictable.
- 🏆 **Gamified Scoreboard**: Track wins, losses, draws, and streaks!
- 🌌 **Futuristic Animations**: Enjoy neon effects, animated previews, and interactive visuals.
- 📝 **Jupyter Notebook**: Easily run, modify, and extend the game in `rockpaperScissorGame.ipynb`.
- 🕹️ **Beginner Friendly**: Clean, well-commented Python for easy learning and hacking.
- 🔥 **Customizable**: Tweak rules, add powerups, or design your own themes!

---

## 🛠️ **How To Play**

1. **Clone the Repo:**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/Ibu0555/RockpaperScissorsGame.git
   cd RockpaperScissorsGame
   ```

2. **Launch The Game:**
   - Open `rockpaperScissorGame.ipynb` in Jupyter Notebook.
   - Hit **Run All** (or step through each cell).
   - Select your move: 🪨 Rock | 📄 Paper | ✂️ Scissors
   - Watch the animated results and your score update in real-time!

3. **Level Up:**
   - Try to beat your best streak.
   - Modify the code to challenge a friend, add powerups, or introduce wildcards!

## 🧠 **How It Works**

- **Player Move:** Prompted for rock, paper, or scissors.
- **AI Move:** Computer picks randomly.
- **Winner Calculation:** Standard rules apply:
  - 🪨 Rock crushes ✂️ Scissors
  - ✂️ Scissors cut 📄 Paper
  - 📄 Paper covers 🪨 Rock
- **Results:** Animated outcome + updated scoreboard.

> **Tip:** All logic is inside the Jupyter Notebook – perfect for learning, exploring, and hacking!
